Our Story, Vision & Team

Our Story

Travel to Tanzania Adventure & Safaris was born from a deep love for the wild beauty of East Africa. Our founder started this journey with a simple dream — to share the magic of Tanzania with the world.

Starting as a professional safari guide and Kilimanjaro guide, our founder gained years of hands-on experience learning how the best tour operators work, what travelers truly want, and how to deliver authentic, life-changing experiences.

The company was officially registered in Tanzania as a licensed tour operator, and from that very first day, the response from travelers was overwhelming — positive remarks and repeat visits became the norm. Today, Travel to Tanzania is a top-rated company serving hundreds of travelers each year from every corner of the world.

Our focus is tailor-made tours with a responsible approach — personalized, enriching experiences that benefit the local community, wildlife and conservation efforts of Tanzania.

Our Responsibilities

We operate with a strong sense of duty — to our clients, our crew, our community and our environment.

  • Ensure client satisfaction on every trip
  • Offer flexible packages for all budgets and preferences
  • Prioritize safety and high standards of service
  • Fair and ethical treatment of all porters, cooks and guides
  • Customize trips to give each client their ideal experience
  • Guarantee punctual departures regardless of group size
  • Respect nature, environment and wildlife conservation
  • Support local communities through volunteering and education
  • Leave no waste behind on any trek or safari
  • Create local employment opportunities for guides and porters

Giving Back to the Community

Travel to Tanzania has always been aware of the need to share the benefits of tourism with the Tanzanian community. We support local communities through fair wages, healthcare, education, food relief, environmental conservation and charitable outreach programs. By booking with us, you directly help improve the lives of people in the communities we operate in.
