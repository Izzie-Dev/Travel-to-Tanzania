About Us

We Create Memorable Journeys

Travel to Tanzania Adventure & Safaris is a locally owned tour company based in Arusha, Tanzania. Our team of passionate travel professionals have been crafting unforgettable African experiences — from the wild Serengeti to the white sands of Zanzibar — for over a decade.

We specialize in wildlife safaris, Kilimanjaro trekking, Zanzibar holidays, cultural tours and day trips — all tailored to your budget and preferences. Your safety, satisfaction and a smile on your face is our highest priority.
